Some platform related Questions

How Long Data is stored once subscription expired?

Your data will be stored for up to 60 days prior to deletion, with in that time if you renew, then you will also get any intermidiate data sent to server

Post 60 days, once data is deleted, it cannot be restored

Which Inverters are supported?

We have done integrations to all major inverters like Delta, SMA, KACO, Su-Kam etc. Most of these inverters have Modbus protocol.

However it will not be difficult to integrate with any other inverter with our self-service REST API's
